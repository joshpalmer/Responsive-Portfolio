About Me

Originally from North Carolina, I graduated from the University of North Carolina Asheville in 2007 with a BA in Economics. I just recently completed an intensive Full Stack Web Development program at the University of California Irvine.

I am passionate about non-profits and giving back to my community. In 2018, I founded the Carolyn H. Palmer Foundation in honor of my late mother. The Foundation's sole mission is to improve the lives of teachers in the State of North Carolina, because my mom was a teacher for 30 plus years.
