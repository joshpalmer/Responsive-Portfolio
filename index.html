About Me

Josh Palmer is a detailed Financial Analyst seeking a position within a well established company. Josh began his career at Accenture Consulting and helped lead a Mutual Fund Accounting outsourcing project which outperformed expectations. His stregth's are cost analysis and finding answers that require the right data and the ability to find it. Josh hold's a bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of North Carolina, Asheville and has taken continuing education courses in JavaScript at the University of Califoria, Irvine. Josh is currently scheduled to take the CFA Level 1 exam in December 2018.

In 2018, Josh founded the Carolyn H. Palmer Foundation in honor of his late mother. The Foundation's sole mission is to improve the lives of teachers in the State of North Carolina. Josh's mother was a teacher for 30+ years and he saw first hand the struggle that teachers endure. The state of NC is at risk for losing some of the best and brightest minds in education.
